# How to Use Professor Corrects on Native Camp (ELI5)

Hey! Since you're already on Native Camp, here's how to let the Professor do the heavy lifting while you keep your competitive advantage.

### 1. The Setup (Before the student joins)
- **Open two windows side-by-side:** Native Camp on one side, and Professor Corrects on the other.
- **Toggle "Native Camp Mode":** Check the box in the Professor's UI to keep things clean.
- **Enter Student Name:** Type their name in the sidebar as soon as you see it on Native Camp.

### 2. The Introduction (The Handover)
- When the lesson starts, **don't wait.**
- Type a quick greeting to the Professor in the "Teacher Introduction" box (e.g., *"Dear Professor, this is Somchai. We're working on his business presentation for a US client. Please help him sound more American."*).
- Click **"Start Live Session"**.

### 3. During the 25 Minutes
- **Listen/Watch:** As the student speaks or writes on Native Camp, **copy-paste their tricky sentences** into the Professor's chat box.
- **The Professor Streams:** She will immediately start giving that warm, elite Cambridge feedback.
- **The Pivot:** Simply **read the Professor's words back to the student** or copy-paste the feedback into the Native Camp chat.
- You provide the "British Authority" presence, while the Professor provides the "American Fluency" technical coaching.

### 4. The Grand Finale (End of Session)
- 2 minutes before the time is up, click **"End & Summarize Session"** in the sidebar.
- The Professor will give you a perfect summary + 3 takeaways.
- **Paste these into the Native Camp session report.** You're done!

---
**Why this works:** Native Camp students in Southeast Asia respect the "British" look/sound but want "American" results. You are giving them both.
